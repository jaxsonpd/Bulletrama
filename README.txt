This is a two player game requiring ir line of sight between funkits.

Try to keep an eye on your opponent as you move about the board firing at them by pushing down on the joy stick.
Either dodge their bullets or distroy them by shooting them out of the sky. Start by distroying their protective wall
then first to hit their opponent 3 times wins.
